Welcome to your highly advanced Personal Finance Tracker. Below is everything you need to know to harness its full power.

System Walkthrough Simulation Active

1. AI Receipt Scanner (OCR)

Stop typing expenses manually! On the Expenses page, click the Scan Bill button.

Upload any picture of a handwritten or printed receipt. The system uses a powerful cloud API (OCR.space Engine 2) to scan the image, intelligently extract the Total Amount & Date, and auto-fill your new expense form instantly.

2. No Spend Days (Challenge Mode)

Gamify your savings! Complete a day without logging any expenses. Navigate to the No Spend Days tab.

Click "Mark Today". If you haven't spent money, the system locks it in and increases your streak. If you accidentally log one, you can click Unlock Today to gracefully exit the challenge and repair the ledger.

3. Expense Time Machine

Beat lifestyle creep. Go to the Time Machine tab to dynamically compare this month to a past month.

The system mathematically renders row-by-row comparisons for every category to instantly identify where you are bleeding cash over time.

4. Spending Score Engine

Located on your Dashboard. Every time you log an expense, the engine aggressively compares it against your Monthly Budget.

You are automatically assigned a dynamic grade (A through F). Approaching your budget drops your score linearly. Puncturing it destroys your score exponentially.

5. User Profile Management

Personalize your experience! Navigate to the Profile tab (or click your Avatar in the header).

You can update your display name, link a phone number, and write a short bio about your financial goals. Your display name seamlessly syncs across the whole application.

6. Multi-Language Support (i18n)

The system speaks your language. Go to Settings > Preferences to find the Language dropdown.

Select English, EspaÃ±ol, or Hindi. The entire layout, dashboard, navigation, and core labels will update instantly without needing to refresh the page.

7. Data Export to CSV

Keep offline records. On the Expenses page, click the green Export button near the top left.

The system gathers all currently filtered financial logs and packages them into a cleanly formatted Excel/Numbers compatible CSV file that downloads directly to your device.

8. Budget Planning

Control your spending before it happens using the Budget page.

You can allocate specific monthly limits for every category (Food, Transport, etc.). As you log expenses, the visual progress bars fill up. Approaching 100% of your allocated budget turns the bar red and lowers your Spending Score.

9. Income Management

Track your cash flow! Head over to the Income page to record your incoming cash (Salary, Freelance, Pocket Money).

Just like expenses, you can tag each income source. The Dashboard uses this data against your total expenses to calculate your Net Balance and available funds automatically.

10. Savings Goals

Planning to buy a laptop or take a trip? Go to the Goals tab to track long-term savings.

Create a Goal with a Target Amount. Whenever you save money, log an addition directly to that goal. The widget calculates what percentage of the goal has been achieved and highlights your primary goal strictly on the main Dashboard.

11. Smart Calendar View

Need a bird's-eye view of your finances? Check out the Calendar page.

This intuitive grid plots every single transaction you've made directly onto its corresponding footprint of the month. Perfect for identifying spending clusters, expensive weekends, or recurring bills visually.

12. Advanced Reports & Analytics

Don't just track data, understand it! Open the Reports page for deep-dive analytics.

You can generate custom breakdowns summarizing your category spending using dynamic Donut and Line charts. Easily compare your largest cash drains instantly.
